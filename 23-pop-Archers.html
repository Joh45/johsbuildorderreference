23 pop Archers

This build is about building and being able to produce archers from two ranges upon hitting Feudal, with fletching coming in soon after. It is a 'slower' build as you need to build a mass of archers with fletching for them to be effective. This build is especially effective against less advanced players, and is also worth learning for team games as flank.
