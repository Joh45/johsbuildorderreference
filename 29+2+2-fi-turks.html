Turks 29+2+2 Fast Imperial

Gunpowder units are used in this build as they don't require upgrades, and Turks can produce them immediately due to their free chemistry. Turks are therefore one of the few somewhat viable civs for a Fast Imperial - though be aware that this build can usually be countered by more experienced players.
