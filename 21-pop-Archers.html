21 pop Archers

Here are some suggestions for civilizations that are good for a 21 pop archer build. It is certainly not necessary to know all these builds - they are just examples of how you could adjust your build to take advantage of an eco bonus.

Ethiopians

Build Order
6 on sheep 6
3 on wood 3 6
1 lure boar, then +1 on sheep 3 8
4 on berries 3 12
+2 on boar 3 14
Rest to wood [2nd LC] 6 14
Loom
Advancing to Feudal
↑10 on wood, 4 on stragglers, 2 to gold 14 4 2
Build barracks
Feudal
Build 2 ranges, make archers
Double-bit axe
↑8 on gold, 4 on stragglers back to sheep 10 8 8
⋮
